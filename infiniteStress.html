Infinite Stress

You’re on a mission. A monstrous Ronald McDonald stands before you. As he waves his four mighty arms, you narrowly avoid the onslaught of french fries and start shooting back. No, this isn’t a nightmare; it’s a game!

Infinite Stress is a game created for the 2020-2021 FIRST Robotics Competition, "Infinite Recharge". This game was developed by three FRC members, one from Team 702, and two from Team 589. This game served as a "pit game" – a display located in the team booths during the annual regional competitions. Infinite Stress was shown at two different competitions, reaching hundreds of other robotics members.

Made with Unity.
